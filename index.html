How to play

You have been personally selected by the Tsar Nicholas II to hunt and capture the Communists before mass exodus.

The Communists are hiding and you will need to outwit them to capture them.

Once they are all captured we have won the war.

This is a memory game, you will need to flip the cards and match the communists together!

8 fails and game over!
